Privacy Policy

Zetonic does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.

Data Storage

All preferences are stored locally in your browser using localStorage. No external servers are used for your data.

External Requests

Video URLs, quote API (Advice Slip), weather (Open-Meteo), and search (Google/DuckDuckGo) may send requests when you use those features. Your settings and custom videos stay on your device.

Cookies

No tracking cookies. The extension does not track users.
