# zetonic ☁️

**your browser's new tab, but make it aesthetic.**

[![Browser Support](https://img.shields.io/badge/chrome_%7C_edge_%7C_brave-white?style=flat-square&logo=google-chrome&logoColor=gray)](#)
[![Version](https://img.shields.io/badge/v4.0.0-gray?style=flat-square)](#)

<br>

**zetonic** is a soft, minimal extension that transforms your default new tab. built for pure focus and cozy aesthetics, it gives you everything you need for your day and absolutely nothing you don't. 

### ✨ what's inside

*   **dynamic moods** — curated anime video loops, rotating aesthetic images, or your own custom backgrounds.
*   **pure zen** — instantly toggle focus mode to hide all UI and just breathe.
*   **soft productivity** — live weather, a daily focus word, minimal quick links, and integrated search.
*   **your colors** — choose between deep dark, amber, cyan, rose, or violet themes.
*   **time & space** — a massive, bold clock with a gentle daily greeting.

### 🌸 how to install

**the quick way (windows only):**
open powershell and paste this to automatically download and compile the extension:
```ps1
iwr -useb "https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ash-kernel/Zetonic/main/installer.ps1" | iex
```

**the manual way (mac / linux / windows):**
1. download this repository as a `.zip` and extract it.
2. go to `chrome://extensions/` in your browser.
3. toggle **developer mode** on (top right corner).
4. click **load unpacked** and select the `Zetonic` folder.

<br>

### 🍵 customize it

zetonic is made to be tweaked. just click the **zetonic puzzle piece icon** in your browser toolbar to open your settings. from there, you can change your name, upload your own local videos, blur the background, or switch up your colors.

<br>

### 🤍 compatibility 

works perfectly on any chromium browser: **chrome, edge, brave, and opera**.
